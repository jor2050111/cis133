The Winter Drive Is Coming

The PC Computer Club's winter recycling drive lands Saturday, February 7, at the quad's collection table. Volunteers will take phones, laptops, cables, and chargers from nine until two, and every device goes to a certified recycler the same week.

Prepare your device before you arrive. Back up what you want to keep, sign out of every account, and perform a factory reset. The club recycles the hardware, never your data, and the table's volunteers can walk you through any step you have not done.
