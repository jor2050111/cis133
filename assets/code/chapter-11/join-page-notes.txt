=====================================================================
PC COMPUTER CLUB: JOIN PAGE NOTES
From the club officers, Fall 2026
=====================================================================

The events page shipped, and it already answers the question we
hear most. Thank you. Now build the second page from our expansion
brief: the join page.

Here is the problem it solves. At every drive, interested students
give an officer their name on a scrap of paper. The scraps get
lost. We want the site to collect each student's name and email so
an officer can invite them to the next meeting. No scraps.

---------------------------------------------------------------------
WHAT THE FORM MUST COLLECT
---------------------------------------------------------------------

REQUIRED. The form cannot do its job without these two.

* Full name.
* Email address. This is how we invite people, so it has to be a
  working address.

OPTIONAL. A student can skip all of these and still join the list.

* How they heard about the club. Give them one menu with these
  choices:
    - A flyer
    - A friend
    - A campus event
    - A web search
    - Other
* What they are interested in. Let them pick any, all, or none:
    - Drop-off days
    - Fix-it workshops
    - The fall drive
    - General meetings
* Questions for the officers. A box where they can write a sentence
  or two if they want. Most will leave it empty, and that is fine.

---------------------------------------------------------------------
AND NOTHING ELSE
---------------------------------------------------------------------

No birthdays. No phone numbers. No student IDs. An officer only
needs a name to greet someone and an email to reach them. Every
extra question we ask is one more reason to walk away from the
form, and one more piece of someone's information we would have to
guard.

Put this sentence on the page, near the form, where every visitor
can read it before they type:

    We use what you share here only to email you about club
    events.

---------------------------------------------------------------------
ONE MORE THING
---------------------------------------------------------------------

The join page should match the rest of the site: same colors, same
logo, same navigation bar. Add it to the menu on every page so a
visitor can join from anywhere.

Send questions to computer-club@example.org. The officers meet
Tuesdays.

END OF NOTES
