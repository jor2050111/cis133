HEADLINE (the page's one big heading)
---------------------------------------------------------------------

The PC Computer Club

---------------------------------------------------------------------
WHAT THE CLUB IS (one paragraph, right under the headline)
---------------------------------------------------------------------

We are students who keep electronics out of the landfill. The club
collects unwanted phones, laptops, cables, and chargers from
campus, and every donation goes to a certified recycler. No major
required, no experience required.

---------------------------------------------------------------------
WHAT WE DO (three short blocks, each with its own small heading)
---------------------------------------------------------------------

RECYCLING DRIVES
Twice a year the club takes over a corner of campus and collects
everything students no longer want. The spring drive brought in
154 items in one Saturday morning.

FIX-IT WORKSHOPS
Every donated device gets tested and wiped before the recycler
picks it up. Members learn to do both at our workshops, and nobody
arrives knowing how.

CLUB MEETINGS
We meet weekly during the semester to plan drives, sort donations,
and welcome whoever walks in.
