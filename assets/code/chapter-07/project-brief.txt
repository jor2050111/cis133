=====================================================================
PC COMPUTER CLUB: SITE EXPANSION BRIEF
From the club officers, Fall 2026
=====================================================================

You built us a three-page site, and it works. The recycling guide
answers the questions we used to answer one email at a time. The
gallery shows what a drive looks like. The contact page fills our
meetings. Thank you.

Now the club is growing, and the site has to grow with it. This
brief tells you what we need. You decide how to build it. Show us
a plan before you build anything.

---------------------------------------------------------------------
WHAT THE SITE MUST ACCOMPLISH (THE CLUB'S GOALS)
---------------------------------------------------------------------

1. Keep electronics out of the landfill. More visitors should find
   our drop-offs and drives, and arrive with devices ready to hand
   over.

2. Grow the club. We want ten new members by December, and the site
   should do the recruiting we cannot do in person.

3. Answer questions before they become emails. Meeting times, event
   dates, and what we accept should be findable without writing to
   an officer.

---------------------------------------------------------------------
WHO VISITS THE SITE (TWO AUDIENCES)
---------------------------------------------------------------------

* Current members. They already know what the club is. They visit
  for one reason: when and where is the next event. They want that
  answer fast, from any page.

* Prospective members. They arrive from a flyer, a friend, or a
  campus search. They need to learn what the club does, see that we
  are welcoming, and find out how to join. If the first page they
  see confuses them, we lose them.

---------------------------------------------------------------------
WHAT WE NEED
---------------------------------------------------------------------

KEEP these three pages. They earn their place.

* The recycling guide (recycling-guide.html)
* The spring drive gallery (drive-gallery.html)
* The contact page (contact.html)

ADD three more.

1. A home page. The site has never had one. A first-time visitor
   lands on the recycling guide and has to guess what the club is.
   We want a front door: what the club is, what we do, and where to
   go next.

2. An events page. Visitors keep asking when the next drop-off is.
   Publish our fall schedule. Here is the raw content, straight
   from the officers' planning notes:

   * Fall Welcome Meeting. Tuesday, September 1, 3:00 to 4:30 p.m.,
     Computer Commons. New members welcome, no device required.
   * September Drop-Off Day. Tuesday, September 15, 3:00 to
     4:30 p.m., Computer Commons. Bring phones, laptops, cables,
     and chargers.
   * October Drop-Off Day. Tuesday, October 13, 3:00 to 4:30 p.m.,
     Computer Commons. Bring phones, laptops, cables, and chargers.
   * Fix-It Workshop. Saturday, October 24, 10:00 a.m. to noon,
     Computer Commons. Learn to test and wipe donated devices.
   * Fall Recycling Drive. Saturday, November 7, 9:00 a.m. to
     1:00 p.m., Student Union lawn. Our biggest event of the term.
   * Semester Wrap Meeting. Tuesday, November 17, 3:00 to
     4:30 p.m., Computer Commons. Drive results and spring plans.

3. A join page. We meet interested students at every drive and then
   lose track of them. We want the site to collect the name and
   email of every student who wants to hear from us, so an officer
   can invite them to the next meeting.

---------------------------------------------------------------------
WHAT WE ARE NOT ASKING FOR
---------------------------------------------------------------------

Do not redesign the club's look. The colors and the logo stay. We
like that every page already matches. Whatever you add should match
too.

Send your plan to computer-club@example.org. The officers meet
Tuesdays and will review it the week you send it.

=====================================================================
END OF BRIEF
