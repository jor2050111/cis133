Drive Day Notes

The spring drive opened at nine with a line of students already waiting at the welcome table. The first hour brought a steady stream of phones, tablets, and one desktop tower that arrived in a wheelbarrow. Volunteers logged every item at the sorting station, and the cable bin needed emptying twice before noon. Nobody predicted that cables and chargers would beat every other category by lunchtime.

The afternoon slowed enough for the crew to rotate breaks and restock the label plates. A late rush around two brought the day's only handheld games, and the recycler's truck arrived on schedule at four. The final tally reached 154 items, and the officers agreed on one change for the fall drive: more bins, fewer boxes, and a second volunteer at the check-in table.
