CIS133 SKILLS LAB 4A: THE CLUB'S DESIGN SET PALETTE
=====================================================================

Every color below comes straight from the club's illustration set,
the same set that drew the logo, the charts, and the drive scenes.
The hex values are exact. Copy them from this file into your CSS
instead of retyping them from memory.

The labels in [BRACKETS] name each color. They are notes to you.
They never go into your stylesheet.

---------------------------------------------------------------------

[CLUB TEAL]
#268080
The club's brand color: the logo disk and every banner in the set.
A strong region background behind white text.

[DEEP TEAL]
#1a5e5e
The darker teal from the logo's shading. A heading color on light
backgrounds, or a background behind white text.

[SUNSET ORANGE]
#f4a259
The sky in every outdoor scene. A warm accent background behind
dark text.

[LIGHT SAND]
#fac78d
The set's palest tone, from the sunlit sky. A page background that
keeps dark text readable.

[SAND]
#deb887
The desert ground under every scene. A calm region background
behind dark text.

[SAGUARO GREEN]
#5e9959
The cactus and bin green. An accent for small touches. Too light to
sit behind white body text.

[INK]
#333333
The dark gray the set uses for text and outlines. The site's body
text color.

[WHITE]
#ffffff
The label plates and the logo's arrows. A page background, or the
text color on either teal.

The set also mixes two browns for background props. Both sit close

these two is a design decision, not a coin flip.
